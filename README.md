**Bank Risk Analytics Dashboard**  
A professionally designed, end-to-end analytics solution that transforms raw banking data into actionable business intelligence for risk assessment and strategic decision-making.

This dashboard delivers a 360° view of banking operations—clients, loans, deposits, and financial activity—enabling stakeholders to identify risk patterns, monitor performance, and make confident, data-driven decisions.

**Tech Stack**  
- Power BI – Interactive dashboard development and storytelling  
- Power Query – Data cleaning, shaping, and transformation  
- DAX – Advanced measures, KPIs, and calculated columns  
- MySQL Workbench – Data extraction and structured querying  
- Microsoft Excel / CSV – Source data preparation  
- Canva – Professional UI templates and background design  
- Windows OS – Development environment  

**Data Source**  
- Dataset: Banking customer, account, loan, and deposit records  
- Source: Public / simulated banking dataset  
- Structure includes:  
  - Customer attributes (ID, Gender, Joining Year, Nationality)  
  - Account details (Checking, Savings, Business Accounts)  
  - Loan information (Bank Loan, Business Lending, CC Balance)  
  - Financial metrics (Deposits, Fees, Foreign Currency Amounts)  

The data is modeled to reflect real-world banking systems, enabling realistic analysis and business-ready insights.

**Features / Highlights**

Banks manage complex, high-volume financial data. Without a centralized and visual analytics layer, identifying high-risk customers, monitoring loan exposure, and tracking deposit performance becomes inefficient and error-prone.

This dashboard is built to:  
- Provide a unified view of customers, loans, and deposits  
- Identify high-risk and high-value customer segments  
- Track core KPIs such as total clients, loans, deposits, and balances  
- Enable deep, interactive analysis by gender, year, and banking relationship  
- Deliver insights in a format that is intuitive for business users  

Key visuals include:  
- KPI cards for instant visibility into total clients, loans, deposits, and balances  
- Navigation buttons for seamless movement between Home, Loan Analysis, Deposit Analysis, and Summary  
- Bar and donut charts showing loan and deposit distribution by income, occupation, and locality  
- Interactive filters for Joining Year, Gender, Banking Relationship, and Advisor  

**Business Impact & Insights**  
- Accelerates identification of high-risk customer segments  
- Reveals patterns in lending and deposits across demographics  
- Supports smarter credit policies and customer engagement strategies  
- Replaces static reporting with an interactive, executive-ready analytics experience
